Assignment title: Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

Prompt: A climate change activism mural made with acrylic spraypaint in a busy city. Diverse protesters hold signs like “Protect Our Planet” and “Climate Justice.” Bold green and blue tones, raised fists, determined faces. Painted on a prominent wall where crowds stop, point, and discuss. Expressive, powerful street art style.

Vocabulary breakdown: Medium: Materials, tools, and processes used to make the artwork. Meaning: Main message. Subject Matter: Whats in the art piece, what we can see. Representational Art: style where we can recognize the shapes and figures in the composition
